# Example Prompts for AI CAD Generator

## Basic Shapes

1. "Make a cube with 50mm sides"
2. "Design a cylinder 30mm diameter and 100mm height"
3. "Create a sphere with radius 25mm"

## Mechanical Components

4. "Create a 120 mm long shaft with a 20 mm diameter and add a 4 mm keyway"
5. "Design a rectangular plate 100x50x5 mm with four 6mm holes at the corners"
6. "Make a bracket with a 90-degree bend, 50mm on each side, 3mm thick"

## Complex Shapes

7. "Create a hollow cylinder with outer diameter 40mm, inner diameter 30mm, and height 60mm"
8. "Design a box 80x60x40mm with a 10mm radius fillet on all edges"
9. "Make a gear with 20 teeth, 50mm diameter, and 10mm thickness"

## Multi-Part Assemblies

10. "Create a simple hinge with two parts connected by a cylindrical pin"
11. "Design a T-joint connector for 20mm diameter pipes"
12. "Make a simple clamp with a screw mechanism"

## Tips for Better Results

- Be specific with dimensions and units (mm is assumed by default)
- Mention geometric relationships clearly (e.g., "centered", "at corners", "aligned")
- For complex shapes, break them down into simpler components
- Specify operations like holes, cuts, fillets, chamfers explicitly
- Use clear, engineering-focused language
